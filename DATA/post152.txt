## Body:
<p>Let $p_n$ be the $n$-th prime. By the <a href="http://primes.utm.edu/glossary/page.php?sort=PrimeNumberThm">Prime Number Theorem</a>,
$$
p_n\sim n\log n\;.
$$
It follows that
$$
\sum_n\frac{1}{p_n(\log p_n)^\alpha}
$$
converges if and only if $\alpha&gt;0$.</p>
