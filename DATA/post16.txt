## Body:
<p>It depends on the level.</p>

<p>For an undergraduate interested in <strong>algebraic</strong> number theory, I would strongly suggest (parts of) Serre's <em>Cours d'arithmetique</em> and also Samuel's <em>Théorie algébriques des nombres</em>.</p>

<p>For a graduate student aiming at a future of research work in number theory, Cassels &amp; Fröhlich is a must.</p>
