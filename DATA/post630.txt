## Body:
<p>For a square lattice, if you're counting the number of lattice points inside a polygon whose vertices are lattice points, then you can use <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pick%27s_theorem" rel="nofollow">Pick's theorem</a>.  Other relevant theorems are <a href="http://mathworld.wolfram.com/BlichfeldtsTheorem.html" rel="nofollow">Blichfeldt's theorem</a> and the <a href="http://mathworld.wolfram.com/MinkowskiConvexBodyTheorem.html" rel="nofollow">Minkowski Convex Body theorem</a>.</p>
