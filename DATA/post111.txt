## Body:
<p>Sequence $\log n$ is not equidistributed. The reason is $\log[n]$ grows very slowly and so $\{\log n\}$ concentrates to the lower part of $[0,1]$. Since $\log\log n!$ grows approximately as $\log(n\log n)$ probably the same proof will be valid.</p>
