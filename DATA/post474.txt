<p>According to <a href="http://oeis.org/A002479">OEIS</a>,</p>

<blockquote>
  <p>$n$ belongs to this sequence iff every prime $ p \equiv 5,7 \pmod 8$ dividing $n$ occurs to an even power. </p>
</blockquote>

<p>So the answer to my question "Is there a theorem analogous to the one about $a^2+b^2$?" is "Yes".</p>
