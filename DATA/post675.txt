## Body:
<p>Faltings' theorem tells us the number of rational points on a smooth quartic plane curve is finite.</p>

<p><strong>Question</strong>: Is there any software for computing all the rational points over $\mathbb{Q}$ on a given (smooth) quartic plane curve?</p>
