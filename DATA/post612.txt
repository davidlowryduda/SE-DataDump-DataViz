## Body:
<p>You are looking for $x\in\mathbb Z_2$ such that $(1+2x)^2=8b+1$. This is equivalent to 
$$ x^2+x-2b=0.$$ 
Now mod $2$, this polynomial is $x(x+1)$ and has (at least) one <b>simple root</b> in $\mathbb F_2$. By Hensel's lemma, the above equation has a solution in $\mathbb Z_2$. </p>
