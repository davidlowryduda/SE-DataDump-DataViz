<p>Check out Chapter 1 of Johan Bosman's Ph.D. thesis available at his homepage</p>

<p><a href="http://homepages.warwick.ac.uk/~masjam/" rel="nofollow">http://homepages.warwick.ac.uk/~masjam/</a></p>

<p>He discusses modular forms and modular curves. </p>

<p>Modular forms are functions on the complex upper half-plane satisfying certain conditions and modular curves are obtained as quotients of the complex upper half-plane by congruence subgroups.</p>
