## Title:
Generators for $\mathbb F_p^*$

## Score:

## Body:
<p>Let $p$ be prime, then it is a well-known fact that $\mathbb F_p^*= \mathbb F_p -\{0\}$ is a cyclic group under multiplication. Are there any methods to determine the generators of this cyclic or any results which help in actually finding the generators?</p>
