## Body:
<p>The number of prime divisors counted with multiplicity is maximized for powers of $2$ and so</p>

<p>$$\Omega(n)\le\frac{\log n}{\log 2}=\log_2 n$$</p>

<p>and since it is exactly equal for infinitely many $n$ it is also the tighest possible bound.</p>
