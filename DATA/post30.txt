## Body:
<p>If $2^a3^b \lt N, a \ln 2 + b \ln 3 \lt \ln N$ or $a+\frac{b \ln 3}{\ln 2}\lt \frac{\ln N}{\ln 2}$.  For large $N$, we can ignore +1s, so to count the number less than $N$, we have $$\sum_{i=0}^\frac{\ln N}{\ln 3}\frac{\ln N-i\ln 3}{\ln 2}=\frac{(\ln N)^2}{\ln 2 \ln 3}-\frac{(\ln N)^2}{2\ln 2 \ln 3}$$ which is within a constant of the Ramanujan result.</p>
